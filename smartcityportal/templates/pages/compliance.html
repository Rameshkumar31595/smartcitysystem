Compliance

Last updated: February 2025

Regulatory Compliance

The Smart City Portal operates in compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing civic technology platforms. We adhere to data protection standards, accessibility requirements, and government digital service guidelines.

Data Protection

We comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA). All personal data collected through this platform is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.

Accessibility

Our platform is designed to be accessible to all citizens, including those with disabilities. We follow WCAG 2.1 guidelines to ensure equitable access to city services and issue reporting features.

Security Standards

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL), secure authentication mechanisms, regular security audits, and responsible vulnerability disclosure practices.

Grievance Redressal

For any compliance-related concerns or grievances, please contact our Compliance Officer at compliance@smartcityportal.in or call our helpline.
