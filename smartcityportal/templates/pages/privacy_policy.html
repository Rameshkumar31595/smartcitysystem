Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Information We Collect

When you register on the Smart City Portal, we collect basic personal information including your name, email address, and username. When you report an issue, we collect the issue details, location, and any images you upload.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Smart City Portal services
  • Process and track your reported civic issues
  • Communicate status updates about your reports
  • Improve our platform and city services
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

Data Sharing

Your reported issues and their details are shared with relevant city departments and authorities for resolution. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data stored on our platform. You can update your profile information at any time through your account settings, or contact us to request data deletion.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@smartcityportal.in.
